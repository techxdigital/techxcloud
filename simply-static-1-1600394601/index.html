From ground to cloud

We believe that digital transformation (DX) is the future going forward for local enterprises in the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

We partner with local enterprises to build, scale and maintain critical business solutions using cloud-based technology.

We empower your employees with the means for making the right decisions by equipping them with the necessary knowledge and skillsets to excel at their jobs in this Digital Age.

about TechX

TechX was founded to support enterprises in the digital transformation journey. This shift is being powered by a new wave of technology that allows companies of all shapes and sizes to be more nimble and agile. With a combined experience of more than a hundred years in the IT industry, the company’s founders have unparalleled insights in this digitalized trend and are joined by a daring and inspiring team. Our specialists consist of great Domain Experts, Solution Architects, Data Scientists and Engineers.
